💸 How to Buy $ALPH?

You can buy Alephium ($ALPH) easily using either a decentralized or centralized exchange. Here's how:

Option 1: Buy through a Decentralized Exchange (DEX)

  1. Go to Uniswap and connect your wallet.
  2. Swap USDT for ALPH using the Ethereum token address: 0x590F820444fA3638e022776752c5eEF34E2F89A6
  3. Use the official Alephium Bridge to transfer your ALPH to the Alephium network.

Option 2: Buy through a Centralized Exchange (CEX)

You can purchase ALPH directly with USDT on any of the following exchanges:

  • Gate.io
  • BitMart
  • MEXC
  • XeggeX
  1. Create an account on your chosen exchange.
  2. Deposit USDT and use it to buy ALPH via the ALPH/USDT pair.
  3. Withdraw your ALPH to your personal Alephium wallet for safekeeping.

Final Step: Transfer ALPH to Your Alephium Wallet

  1. Open your Alephium wallet and click “Receive” to get your address.
  2. Send your ALPH from the exchange or external wallet to your Alephium address.
  3. Your ALPH should appear in your wallet shortly after the transaction is processed.
