🧬 Lore

Owlphium is a humanoid owl from the planet Noctavium, located in the GX Andromedae system, just 10 light years from Earth. His civilization, 400 years ahead in tech, lives in harmony with nature, dwelling in massive living trees and practicing quantum-code rituals.

While exploring the universe for decentralized technologies, Owlphium discovered Bitcoin, then Ethereum, and ultimately Alephium — a blockchain so elegant, he chose to stay on Earth and build something revolutionary.

He was later joined by Cluckmaster, a fellow Noctavium citizen from the chicken race. Together, they created $OWLP to inspire memes, decentralization and owl culture across the cryptosphere.
